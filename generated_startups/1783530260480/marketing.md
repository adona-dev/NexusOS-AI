# Marketing Strategy Sheet: SmartGrow AI

## 1. Marketing Campaigns
* Campaign Name: "Perfect soil moisture, 24/7."
* Strategy: Content marketing on YouTube showcasing plant growth comparing standard watering vs smart Grow spikes.

## 2. Target Ad Hooks
* Hook 1: "Never guess when to water your plants again."
* Hook 2: "Reduce your crop water bills by 40% with smart IoT irrigation spikes."

## 3. Video Storyboard Script
* Frame 1: Dry soil spike triggers alert. Red warning "Moisture low: 22%".
* Frame 2: Solenoid water valve opens automatically. Reservoir drops slightly.
* Frame 3: Moisture rises to 75%. Plant grows dynamically.
