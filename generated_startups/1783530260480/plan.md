# Product Execution Roadmap: SmartGrow AI

## 1. Business Model Canvas
* Value Proposition: Automated, smart soil moisture control to optimize crop yield & conserve water.
* Cost Structure: Soil sensors production, Wi-Fi telemetry routing modules.

## 2. Revenue & Pricing Tiers
* Garden Spike Kit: $49 hardware + free local Wi-Fi monitoring.
* Pro Farm Multi-Node: $249 hardware + $5/mo cloud irrigation forecast reports.

## 3. Launch Timeline
* Month 1: Design capacitive moisture probes.
* Month 3: Greenhouse testing cycles.
* Month 6: Launch public e-commerce store.
