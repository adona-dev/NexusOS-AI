# Market Research: AGrow AI (Smart Irrigation IoT)

## 1. Target Demographics & User Needs
* Professional crop growers, urban balcony farmers, and greenhouse managers.
* Key Needs: Soil moisture telemetry feedback, reservoir volume alarms, automatic irrigation triggers.

## 2. Competitive Audit
* Legacy timers: Water blindly regardless of rain forecasting.
* Smart spikes: Battery life under 3 months, lack multi-node support.

## 3. Future Trend Analysis
* Hydroponic nutrient monitoring via IoT telemetry devices (+22% YoY).
